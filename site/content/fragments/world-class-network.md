If you can take out your phone and reach out to three people performing at the highest level, especially people you've actually worked with or know well enough that they'd respond, then you have a _world class network._

People building businesses at global scale, working on cutting edge science or tech, competing at world championships, creating art or music that reaches global audiences. 

If you can't think of any, then that's something to think about.
